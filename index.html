Xinxin Liu

CS PhD student

E-mail: xinxin@hust.edu.cn


Biography: Xinxin Liu is a PhD student majoring in computer system architecture in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advised by Prof.Yu Hua. She obtained the BE degree in Software Engineering from Central China Normal University (CCNU) in 2016.

Her research interests include power-aware architecture, non-volatile storage system and RDMA performance.
